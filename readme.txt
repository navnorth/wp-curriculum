Manage and display lesson plans and other curriculum with connections to Open Educational Resources (OER) and alignment to academic content standards.

== Description ==
WP Curriculum is a WordPress plugin developed by Navigation North to enable educators to create, manage, and share their lesson  plans and other curriculum easily.


== Installation ==
1. Log in to your site's WordPress Admin Dashboard (e.g. www.yourwebsite.com/wp-admin)
2. Click on the "Plugins" tab in the left panel, then click "Add New".
3. Search for "WP Curriculum" and the latest version will appear at the top of the list of results.
4. Install it by clicking the "Install Now" link.
5. When installation finishes, click "Activate Plugin".
6. Now click the blue button on the top right that says "Setup"
7. Choose to import resources, subject areas, academic standards, and resource thumbnails.
8. Visit the "Settings" page of WP OER to customize styles and display options.
9. Enjoy!
